subjectivity of Mws

description::
· there is no OBJECTIVE-information.
· aLL information is SUBJECTIVE (= relative to a-brain).
· objective is 'the-archetype|the-referent' of information, the-entity mainly outside of brains.
· but ALL information has a-DEGREE of truth|untruth, the-degree of mapping|modeling its referent|archetype correctly.
· the actions, experiments and the-time sometimes reveal its degree of truth.
· THEN information without referring explicitly or implicitly its 'subject' (= the-brain-entity) that supports it, has no meaning or many meanings.
· for example who is "the-enemy" in the-case of two states engaged in a-war?
