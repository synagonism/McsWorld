This page (and its subpages) contains the SUBJECTIVE modelInfoWorld of Kaseluris.Nikos.1959.

modelInfoWorldw-subjectivity: There is no OBJECTIVE-information.
ALL information is SUBJECTIVE (= relative to a-brain).
Objective is 'the-referent' of information, the-entity mainly outside of brains.
But ALL information has a-DEGREE of truth|untruth, the-degree of mapping|modeling its referent correctly.
The actions, experiments and the-time sometimes reveal its degree of truth.
THEN information without referring explicitly or implicitly its 'subject' (= the-brain-entity) that supports it, has no meaning or many meanings.
For example who is "the-enemy" in the-case of two states engaged in a-war?

modelInfoWorld-definedness: The-definition of a-concept is text|speech that UNIQUELY identifies it.
Undefined-information (= without definitions) for all THE-NAMES it employs is a-source of miscommunication.
The-goal of this modelInfoWorld is to have ALL its names defined.
